About The Event

A Night of Gratitude & Worship

Ayeyi Praise is an annual worship gathering that brings together believers from across Ghana to lift up one voice in praise and thanksgiving to our God. "Ayeyi" — meaning praise in Twi — is at the heart of everything we do.

This year's edition, hosted by the anointed Minstrel Comfort Acheampong, promises to be an unforgettable encounter with God's presence. Come expecting miracles, healing, and a deeper connection with the Father.
